About Us

Welcome to the Crystal Palace

Built in 2003 and refurbished in 2018, Crystal Palace is a four star hotel conveniently located in the most important business area
in Northern Bucharest, only 10 km away from Otopeni International Airport and 15 minutes from the city's historical center.

Crystal Palace Hotel is located in the heart of Bucharest's most important business district and near the Promenada Mall. Within
a radius of 5 km you’ll find RomExpo - the largest Exhibition Center, Romaero, Pipera district, Floreasca district with the most
important business parks, House of Free Press Square and Charles de Gaulle Square.

WE CARE | Your safety at Crystal Palace Hotel

“Crystal Palace Hotel’s hospitality culture consists in people caring for people. We assure you that we are welcoming our guests in the cleanest and healthiest conditions possible.” Bogdan Dragomir - CEO Crystal Palace Hotel

Our top priorities are our guests’ and colleagues’ safety, protection and comfort.

We’ve joined the entire world’s fight in preventing COVID-19’s spreading and we’ve trained our people, taking all necessary
measures to make sure that, as always, our guests’ comfort and stay are satisfied.

For our guests' comfort and safety, we have implemented new cleaning standards for the entire building, using the most efficient
hygienic substances, to make sure that we are respecting our responsability in following all measures recommended by the
World Health Organization.

Peaceful and Quiet

Peaceful and quiet, a retreat from the city bustle, Crystal Palace Hotel has 74 spacious and comfortable rooms. Our 11 suites,
designed to create the perfect balance between relaxation and a suitable working environment.

Due to our strategic location, high quality of services, conference and business facilities, guest rooms’ diversity and parking
facilities, our hotel can be the ideal solution for business, tourist and groups.

The diversity of our guest rooms, the design and offered facilities comply with all our guests’ demands.
